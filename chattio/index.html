Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Chattio processes all your messages locally on your device. We never store or transmit your chat data to our servers.

Which platforms do you support?

We currently support WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Slack, Discord, Telegram, and more coming soon.

Does it work on Windows or Mac?

Absolutely—macOS is production-ready today, and Windows is available via an open beta for early adopters. Use the Download button above to grab whichever installer fits your setup.

Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your chat messages, contact lists, or media files. All processing of your personal communication data happens locally on your device.

2. Account Information

When you purchase Chattio, we collect your email address and payment information through our secure payment processor. We do not store credit card details.

3. Third-Party Services

Chattio acts as a unified interface for third-party services (e.g., WhatsApp, Slack). Your interactions with these services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g., app crashes, feature usage) to improve the application. You can opt-out of this in the settings.
