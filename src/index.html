Data Handling

Chatterly is designed with privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

Direct Web Integration

Chatterly embeds the official web apps directly in a webview. We don't intercept or modify your communications.

No Credential Storage

Your login credentials are handled entirely by each service. Chatterly never stores your passwords or authentication tokens.

No Message Storage

Messages are never stored or transmitted through a custom server. All communication goes directly to the service providers.

Local Only

All app data and preferences are stored locally on your device. Nothing is synced to external servers.
