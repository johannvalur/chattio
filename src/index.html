Your privacy is our priority. Learn how Chattio protects your data and respects your security.

Data Handling

Chattio is designed with privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

Direct Web Integration

Chattio embeds the official web apps directly in a webview. We don't intercept or modify your communications.

üîë

No Credential Storage

Your login credentials are handled entirely by each service. Chattio never stores your passwords or authentication tokens.

No Message Storage

Messages are never stored or transmitted through a custom server. All communication goes directly to the service providers.

Local Only

All app data and preferences are stored locally on your device. Nothing is synced to external servers.

Telemetry & Analytics

Help us improve Chattio by sharing anonymous usage data. You can opt out at any time.
